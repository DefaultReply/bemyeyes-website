How can YOU help?

Be My Eyes is put on this planet to make a positive impact in the lives of blind and visually impaired people. We rely on volunteers to sustain our operations. So here's how you can help:

Become a sighted volunteer

Be My Eyes could not exist without sighted volunteers. If you have time and an iOS device, download the app and lend your eyes to the blind and visually impaired.

Translate into your language

You can help translate Be My Eyes into your native language. Getting started is easy and unbinding. Click here to start translating.

Donate to the project

If you want to help us reach the 285 million people, who are estimated to be visually impaired worldwide, you can donate to the project. Donate via PayPal or Credit Card.
